# X-Ops Alliance Brand Guidelines

## Brand idea

X-Ops Alliance represents independent communities connected through a neutral coordination layer.

The visual identity should communicate:

- Connection without hierarchy.
- Technical credibility.
- Ecosystem collaboration.
- Autonomy and shared impact.

## Logo concept

The symbol is an X-shaped network mark built from connected paths and independent nodes.

Meaning:

- The X represents X-Ops as a broad operational and technical territory.
- The nodes represent communities, conferences and initiatives.
- The centre represents neutral coordination, not ownership.
- The gradient represents diversity of actors converging into shared impact.

## Typography

Recommended type system:

- Primary UI and body: Inter.
- Fallback: system sans-serif.
- Tone: clear, technical, sober and confident.

## Tone of voice

Use language that is:

- Clear.
- Neutral.
- Collaborative.
- Technically credible.
- Direct without sounding corporate-heavy.

Avoid language that suggests ownership or hierarchy:

- Avoid: parent community, official authority, powered by, owned by.
- Prefer: coordination layer, ecosystem partner, member community, conference partner.

## Correct positioning

Recommended phrase:

> X-Ops Alliance is a neutral coordination layer for independent communities, initiatives and conferences.

Do not present the alliance as:

- A HackerDreams sub-brand.
- A commercial extension of X-Ops Conference.
- A vendor-led programme.
- A federation with authority over members.

## Visual usage

Use the primary logo on:

- Landing pages.
- Decks.
- Reports.
- Partnership proposals.

Use the logo mark on:

- Social avatars.
- Favicons.
- Stickers.
- Small UI surfaces.

Keep generous spacing around the logo. Do not place it over visually noisy backgrounds unless contrast is guaranteed.

## Co-branding rule

When used with communities or conferences, X-Ops Alliance should appear as a coordination or partnership seal, never above or larger than the member's own brand.

Recommended language:

- Member of X-Ops Alliance.
- X-Ops Alliance community partner.
- X-Ops Alliance conference partner.
- In collaboration with X-Ops Alliance.

Avoid:

- Powered by X-Ops Alliance.
- Officially governed by X-Ops Alliance.
- Under X-Ops Alliance.
