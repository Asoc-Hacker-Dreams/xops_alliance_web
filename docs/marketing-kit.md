# X-Ops Alliance Marketing Kit

## Core positioning

**X-Ops Alliance** is a neutral coordination layer for independent technical communities, initiatives and conferences.

It exists to connect the ecosystem, not to absorb it.

## Main tagline

**Independent communities. Shared impact.**

## Secondary taglines

- Coordinate without centralising.
- United tech communities. Shared impact.
- A neutral layer for ecosystem collaboration.
- Communities sustain the network. Conferences amplify it.

## One-line description

X-Ops Alliance connects independent tech communities, initiatives and conferences so they can coordinate calendars, share visibility and strengthen the ecosystem without losing autonomy.

## Short boilerplate - English

X-Ops Alliance is a neutral coordination layer for independent technical communities, initiatives and conferences. It helps the ecosystem coordinate efforts, reduce overlaps, share visibility and create stronger collective impact while preserving the autonomy, identity and governance of every member.

## Short boilerplate - Spanish

X-Ops Alliance es una capa neutral de coordinación para comunidades técnicas, iniciativas y conferencias independientes. Ayuda al ecosistema a coordinar esfuerzos, reducir solapamientos, compartir visibilidad y generar mayor impacto colectivo preservando la autonomía, identidad y gobernanza de cada miembro.

## What we are

- A neutral coordination layer.
- A trust network between communities and conferences.
- A shared visibility and calendar infrastructure.
- A framework for collaboration without hierarchy.

## What we are not

- Not a vendor programme.
- Not a parent community.
- Not a commercial funnel.
- Not a replacement for existing conferences.
- Not a central authority over communities.

## Audience

### Communities

Communities join to gain visibility, avoid duplicated efforts, access speaker opportunities and contribute to a stronger national ecosystem.

### Conferences

Conferences join as resonance partners. They amplify the ecosystem, coordinate with other events and gain legitimacy without losing editorial or commercial independence.

### Initiatives and platforms

Initiatives join as ecosystem infrastructure: education, media, tooling, research, newsletters, directories or shared projects.
