# X-Ops Alliance Founding Text

## English

**X-Ops Alliance** is born as an open and neutral collaboration framework between technical communities, independent initiatives and reference conferences that share one conviction: the ecosystem grows more when it cooperates than when it competes.

Knowledge, experience and talent are distributed. However, efforts are still fragmented. X-Ops Alliance exists to coordinate without centralising, align without imposing and amplify without diluting the identity of those who take part.

This alliance does not belong to any vendor, product or single organisation. It does not replace existing communities or events. It connects them.

Each actor keeps its autonomy, voice and purpose while contributing to a shared map of real impact.

We are guided by three simple principles:

- Stability, to build for the long term.
- Technical judgement, to decide with knowledge and responsibility.
- Coordinated action, to transform ideas into visible outcomes.

Communities sustain the living fabric of the ecosystem.
Conferences make it visible and amplify it.
X-Ops Alliance exists so both can meet, respect one another and become stronger together.

This is not a closed organisation. It is an open invitation to those who want to participate with commitment, collaboration and independence.

The union begins now.
The ecosystem is built together.

Nos guiamos por tres principios simples:

- Estabilidad, para construir a largo plazo.

- Accion coordinada, para transformar ideas en resultados visibles.

Las comunidades sostienen el tejido vivo del ecosistema.
Las conferencias lo hacen visible y lo amplifican.

La union comienza ahora.
El ecosistema se construye juntos.
